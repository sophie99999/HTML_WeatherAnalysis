Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator(0 Deg. Latitude). More increasingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earch at the time of the year this data was gathered.
